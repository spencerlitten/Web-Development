    "I started playing golf when I was 7 years old."



    My Dad would take me a few times a week to the golf course where he taught me how to play, and I quickly developed a solid swing and really good short game. I also watched my sisters compete in high school golf matches and that’s how I got interested in competing. I fell in love with the game and started to practice more and make my Dad take me to the course every day. I played my first tournament on a regulation course at age 9 and finished 10th. I continued to get better and I won a few local junior tournaments at ages 9 and 10.

    I broke 100 at age 10, and broke 80 at age 13 shooting 75 and beating my Dad for the first time.

    I was MVP and scoring champion for Northside Middle School my 7th and 8th grade years.

    I was 3-time overall Junior Club Champion at Harrison Lakes CC, 2010, 2012, 2013, and runner up in 2011, competing against kids older than me.

    At age 11, I placed 5th in a state junior Mountain Dew tournament, this was the start of many more good finishes. As a freshman I made the 2014 Columbus North High School State Champion team and competed in several matches and varsity invitational tournaments.

    In the Carmel Invite, I was team medalist with a 79. In July 2014, I had my first hole-in-one while playing with my Dad and a teammate, very exciting. I worked hard on my game that summer and fall and focused on being more consistent. During the winter of 2014-15, I swam varsity for the North swimming team which increased my strength and stamina, but allowed no time for golf. During Spring break following swim season, we spent a week in Florida where I worked on all aspects of my game, with emphasis on putting and scoring irons. That helped me solidify a varsity position for the 2015 golf season, when in May I fired a career low round of 68 (-4) in a HS match against #1-ranked Center Grove and Franklin which included 7 birdies.

    A summary of my 2015 HS season included

    22 varsity starts, event medalist 3 times, team medalist 5 times, with 13 top 5 event finishes.

    In June, I placed 1st in Indiana Junior 15-16 Age Group at Greensburg CC with a 73, and shot rounds of 71, 80, and 73 in the 2015 Columbus Men’s City Tournament finishing 4th overall. I have continued to improve my game each year, my consistency has improved and my scoring continues to trend lower.

    My other interests include swimming, camping, fishing, travel, church, reading, guitar, and spending time with friends.

    My family includes my Dad, Steve, a former collegiate golfer, my Mom, Lee Ann, sisters Erica, Alaina, and Alex (the latter two, high school golfers at Columbus North)

    Now a junior with a GPA of 3.65/4.0,

    I am focusing on a goal of selecting the right college and golf program by visiting schools and meeting with golf coaches, discussing the prospects of becoming a member of their golf team. It is my desire to be a contributing member of a competitive college golf team while earning my college education.
